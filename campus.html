Proposed Framework

‣Gurukul

Based on ancient India’s 64 forms of arts (KALAS) and 14 source of knowledge (VIDYAS). Designing a Campus for Gurukulam along with Acharyas becomes very important – Moreover, the entire Gurukulam is a Prayoga Shala. Therefore the lifestyle itself has to be built where learning happens naturally. There is no separation between lifestyle and learning period.

‣Goshala

Follows our Ancient India’s joyous and prosperous thinking by serving cow in the form of goshalas. Goshala is a protective shelter for cattle in India, where cattle are respected, venerated and treated with dignity.
