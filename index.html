INTRODUCTION
ātmōnnati edukulam

A Pure Indian Education System through which we want to create a self-content world and make available such circumstances through educational revolution that there will be no preservation of the planet needed and lifestyle itself will be part of the environment.

For whom ‘Gurukul’ system of education For the students between age 8/10 to 16/18 and wanted to learn about all religions, science and technology, various languages including Sanskrit , medicine, philosophy, literature, welfare, astrology, culture , history, and many more , in healthy environment.

Why ‘Gurukul’ system of education?

Gurukul students are polite and orderly. They are taught to follow a well-planned schedule at school. Learners are more focused than ordinary students. This is because they are trained in meditation-like techniques that enhance their concentration. In gurukul, students are taught to respect everyone regardless of race, religion, ethnicity, culture, religion and way of looking at things. As a result, such students grow up to be very well-mannered and morally sound. The ‘guru-shishya parampara’ is an important part of Gurukul. Here the students have deep respect for their teachers and enjoy a good relationship with them. They were formed under the direction of their counsellors. In gurukul, the ‘guru's’ also take full responsibility for their ‘shishya's’. They share their experiences, inculcate good habits, and shape students' character in a positive way. Gurukul students are taught to appreciate the environment. Therefore, they are very close to nature. They worship nature and learn many things from it. Students are spiritually elevated which gives them a hopeful, calm and composed personality.
