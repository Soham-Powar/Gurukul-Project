About us

Vijayalaxmi Charitable Trust based in Kolhapur city and continuously working for betterment of society for the past 5 years. It serves for all aspects of society majorly in the Educational field.  Trust always aims to create personalities and wisdom through providing knowledge venturing platforms for everyone.

Vision and Mission

To provide sustainable solutions for challenges faced in Society, Academia & Profession & generate opportunities globally. Sustainability for us will not only focus on social, economic & environmental concerns but we tend to create innovation through education for generations to come.

Sustainability for us will not only focus on social, economic & environmental concern but we tend to create innovation through education for generations to come. Our motive is to create a self-content world and make available such circumstances through educational revolution that there will be no preservation of planet needed and lifestyle itself will be part of environment.

Objective

To align trust deed in sync with global and national missions of Sustainable Development Goals Outlined by United Nations and Government of India.
